Our Story

We Built the Tool We Wished We Had

Strong Tower AI was founded after watching one too many hardworking business owners spend their evenings on tasks that a well-designed system could handle in seconds.

We saw businesses losing leads because nobody responded fast enough. We saw owners working weekends to catch up on admin. We saw teams wasting hours on copy-and-paste work that should have been automated years ago.

So we set out to build something different — an AI automation partner that speaks plain language, delivers fast results, and prices its services so any growing business can afford to compete.

Today, we help businesses across real estate, healthcare, law, e-commerce, and consulting automate the work that was holding them back — and scale with confidence.
